Zebra facts

  • Baby zebras are born with brown stripes instead of black. They don't change to black until a few months after they are born!
  • In the wild zebras usually live in either non-breeding bachelors groups of only males, with a few females sometimes, in a harem of one male with up to 4 females, or in a herd.
  • There are 3 species of zebra!
    • The plains zebra
    • The mountain zebra
    • The Grevy's zebra
  • Zebras are herbivores, so they only eat plant matter!
  • Young zebras generally stay with their mother until they are around 1 year old, although there are cases of young leaving earlier and later
