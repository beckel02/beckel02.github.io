Baby zebra!

Look at this adorable baby zebra with its mom at Como Zoo . Baby zebras are born with brown stripes and their stripes don't turn black for at least 5 months.

Como Zoo in St. Paul MN has recently welcomed TWO new baby zebras and they can be seen playing in the hoof-stock area of the zoo.
