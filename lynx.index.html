Canadian Lynx Facts!

  • Lynx only weigh around 20lbs. This makes them just a little heavier than your housecat!
  • Lynx are carnivores, so they eat meat.

Baby Canadian Lynx kittens!

The Minnesota Zoo welcomed four new baby Lynx kittens this summer (2014). As of right now the kittens are off sight, but the parents can sometimes be seen on exhibit when they are not taking care of the kittens.
