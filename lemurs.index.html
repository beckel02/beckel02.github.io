Baby ring tailed lemurs

As a child I grew up watching this amazing show called Zaboomafoo this was a show about brothers who helped out animals in need with the assistance of a ring-tailed lemur named Zaboo.

These are two of the four baby lemurs that were born at the Lake Superior Zoo this past summer (2014). I would highly recomend going to see them as they are very playful.

For the first week after being born ring-tailed lemurs stay on their mothers stomach. As they get older they branch out a little and can be seen on the mothers back and then eventually exploring on their own.
